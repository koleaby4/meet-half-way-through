Situation

Let's imagine you want to organise a family event, catchup with friends or a business meeting. Great! The choice of place is obvious - it should be convenient for you, the organiser :) Right?...

Complication

Hm... but what would other people think of your organisational skills?
Yes, remember - the other people, who will have to fly from Canada, USA and New Zealand to meet you at your lively Pyramiden at the north of Norway...

Resolution

We can help you finding central point for the meeting :)

Step 1: Scroll to 'Who is coming' section or select it from top menu.
Step 2: Enter and confirm participants' names and addresses.
Step 3: Scroll to 'Map view' section or select it from top menu to see the central location.
Step 4: Click on the central pin to see address of the place and use map for exploring nearby area for restaurants, hotels, etc.
Step 5: Copy address or take screen shot and send it to the participants. Enjoy you gathering, catch up or meeting :)
